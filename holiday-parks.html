Accommodation :: Holiday Parks

Queenstown offers many accommodation options for those wanting to camp under the stars, or visitors who are travelling around New Zealand by motor home.

For campsite and holiday park accommodation visitors are spoilt for choice, and many options are close to the town’s centre.

Well equipped with laundries, Wi-Fi, kitchen and communal lounges, as well as playgrounds for children, travellers will enjoy the on-site facilities available at Queenstown's holiday parks and camp grounds.

Queenstown Holiday Park Creeksyde

We welcome you to our Award Winning Holiday Park located just metres from the town centre and supermarket

Queenstown TOP 10 Holiday Park

At Queenstown TOP 10 we offer a range of accommodation options including Motels, Self-Contained & Ensuite Units, Cabins & Powered & Non-Powered sites.

Kingston Holiday Park

Affordable motel units, budget cabins, glamping tents, RV & Tent sites (powered or unpowered). Come and relax at the southern end of Lake Wakatipu.

Mrs Woolly's Campground

In a stunning mountain setting, Mrs. Woolly’s Campground offers 36 unpowered camping sites, plus glamping tents, hot showers, BBQs, kitchen & laundry

Lakeland Park Christian Camp

Lakeland Park is a hire facility suitable for large group bookings, situated on the Kelvin Peninsula.
